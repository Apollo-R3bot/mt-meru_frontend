Clinics & General Outpatients

Our Health Centres are critical to the network we have established in Tanzania. By offering medical services within key community clusters across the country, we aim to make high-quality health care more accessible and equitable. Our Health Centres are linked digitally to one another and to our two leading hospitals to offer the people of Tanzania comprehensive and integrated health care services.
