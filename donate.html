Donate now

By choosing to support the Mount Meru Reginal Referral Hospital you are making a difference in the lives of our patients and consumers, their families, carers and countless others within the MMRRH community.

It takes considerable resources to remain at the forefront of modern health care, and that is why we are so grateful to have you on our team. Thank you for advancing our commitment to advance health care for everyone, every day.

Here are just a few ways your donation can make an impact:

  • Investing in world-class research
  • Purchasing innovative equipment and technology
